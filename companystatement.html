B+AC, LLC | Structural Engineers

B+AC ia a full service structural engineering consultant focused on providing practical engineering services and creative solutions. We draw from the depth and breadth of our experiences with respected consuting powerhouses.

About us:

Keeping things simple is within the DNA of our organization. We believe simple solutions to the most complicated problems are the high points of technology and engineering.
Each project is the most important project for its user(s) and owner(s). In turn, it's a priority for our work at B+AC to match this standard and reflect the utmost respect and attention. We remain flexible while respecting the laws of science, society, engineering, and ethics.
Over the years, we have contributed to the successful completion of multiple projects across three continents. Within the United States we have had the opportunity to bring positive influence to multiple projects across 18 states.

MBE/DBE Certification:

B+AC is a certified Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) certification in both Massachusetts and Rhode Island.
B+AC is a certified as Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE) by Massachusetts DOT.

Philosophy:

Solutions: We believe in elegant solutions. We believe in cost effectiveness. We also pursue solutions imbued with consideration toward aesthetics, user experience, schedule, and the financials associated with reaching a structure's full expected lifespan.

Service: We bring to the table experience, skill, ingenuity, resourcefulness, and sincere client care. We also value and embrace a teamwork approach, both welcoming ideas from all corners and contributing with our cumulative knowledge as appropriate.

Serendipities: B+AC is nimble with its engagement, welcoming ideas and the unexpected as potential gateways to more suitable solutions for client needs.
